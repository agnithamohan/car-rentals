Overview

Crazy Taxi is among the first companies in India to focus on car rental, in contrast to earlier companies in India that focused on chauffeur-driven rentals. Crazy Taxi was founded by Agnitha and Akash in 2013 in Bangalore, India. As of February 2017, the company operates a fleet of over 300 vehicles in 4 cities across the country: Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad , Bhubaneswar.
